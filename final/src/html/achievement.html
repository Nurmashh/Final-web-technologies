N&B is an affiliate of Oral Design, a network of leading international professionals practicing dental aesthetics. Founded in 1982 to increase the cooperation and communication between technicians and dentists and improve patients’ oral health and self image.

N&B is a member of The American Academy of Esthetic Dentistry,
an invitation-only membership organization for the most accomplished professionals in the field of esthetic dentistry. Established in 1975, the AAED promotes the integration of dental esthetics into the total spectrum of oral health care.

N&B is a diplomat member of the European Society of Cosmetic Dentistry, which seeks to establish an European wide quality assurance in the area of cosmetic and esthetic procedures in the maxillo-facial, oral and dental field and the esthetic lab procedures.

N&B is an affiliate of The American Society for Dental Aesthetics (ASDA). Founded in 1976 by the “Father of Aesthetic Dentistry”, it is the first national society for cosmetic dentists that was established in the U.S.

N&B is an esteemed fellow of the International Academy for Dental Facial Esthetics. The academy is an honorary service organization whose mission is to foster interdisciplinary education in the area of facial esthetics. The ADFE includes among its membership many outstanding individuals of the various associated professions who have a desire to create new bridges whereby the esthetic patient can be better and more knowledgeably served.
